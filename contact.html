Frequently Asked Questions

When are your live streams?

+

We stream regularly throughout the week, with intensive sessions planned for July through October. Check our streams page for the current schedule and upcoming sessions.

Is all your content really free?

+

Yes! All our core content, including live streams, practice materials, and study guides, is completely free. We believe quality SAT prep should be accessible to everyone.

How can I get personalized help?

+

Join our live streams for real-time Q&A, comment on our YouTube videos, or reach out through our contact form. We try to address individual questions during streams and in follow-up content.

What score improvements can I expect?

+

Score improvements vary significantly based on your starting point, study consistency, and effort. We focus on teaching proven strategies and techniques that can help you maximize your potential on the SAT.

Do you offer one-on-one tutoring?

+

Currently, our focus is on providing high-quality group instruction through live streams. For personalized help, Tarushv also tutors through UPchieve and leads bootcamps at Schoolhouse.world.

How do I suggest topics for streams?

+

Use our contact form above, comment on our YouTube videos, or reach out on Instagram. We love hearing what topics you want to see covered and try to incorporate suggestions into our content.
